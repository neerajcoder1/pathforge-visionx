# NEXUS Wiki Home

This wiki is the authoritative documentation set derived from the PRD.

## What NEXUS Is

NEXUS is an adaptive onboarding intelligence system that combines:

- Probabilistic mastery modeling with Bayesian Knowledge Tracing (BKT).
- Constraint-aware pathway planning with a masked action space.
- Multi-objective optimization for three pathway styles.
- Formal correctness guarantees using Z3.
- Dual-audience explainability for technical and HR stakeholders.

## Core Product Promise

NEXUS replaces static, one-size-fits-all onboarding with personalized, verified learning pathways that maximize competency gain per hour while preserving compliance and prerequisite correctness.

## Design Philosophy

- Honest over hype: deterministic planning over black-box policy claims.
- Verifiable over plausible: formal checks before output.
- Explainable over opaque: every module decision is traceable.
- Demo-safe over fragile: fallback and cache-first architecture.
